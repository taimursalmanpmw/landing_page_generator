Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best way to store chocolates?

A: It's best to store chocolates in an airtight container in a cool, dry place to maintain their freshness and quality.

Q: Can I freeze chocolates?

A: Yes, you can freeze chocolates for up to 6 months. Simply wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil and place them in a freezer-safe bag.

Q: Can I use chocolates in baking?

A: Yes, you can use chocolates in a variety of baked goods, such as cakes, cookies, and brownies. Simply melt the chocolates with a little bit of oil and mix well with your ingredients.
